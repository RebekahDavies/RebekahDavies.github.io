Overview

Due to the difference in skill for students throughout the class, the first year focused on an introduction to software development. However, unlike some other courses, this course had a large focus on having the students work ready as soon as we graduated. This meant having a decent amount of soft skills as well as the development skills. This year set the foundation for the rest of the course, focusing on developing applications as part of a team with experience in full stack development.

The modules based around soft skills focused on working as a team to gather requirements (with an introduction to user stories) and team development with Git source control. The other modules, taught us some basics in terms of front end development with web development and some basic JavaScript,and back end development with Java fundementals and SQLite.

We get real experience practive with a team project at the end of each semester. These projects are real industry projects brought in from external companies in Newport and Cardiff.
